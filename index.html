James Wilkins' Data Science Portfolio 2021

Here is a link to my project page which covers the changing relationship between the macroeconomy and financial markets. here.

Covid-19

The first chart tracks Covid-19 cases by different regions within the UK. On the default chart each region in the UK is split into different colours. Alternatively the user can use the drop down selectors to choose a region and view its data.

Productivity puzzle

The second chart looks at labour productivity within the UK from the early 1970s onwards.

UK Unemployment

My third chart presents the unemployment rate in the UK, of those aged over 16 years old.

1st Covid Vaccine

My fourth chart shows the number of people vaccinated with at least the 1st covid vaccine, taken from the Gov.UK coronavirus API.

Inflation in the USA

My fifth chart shows CPI in the USA from 1960 to 2021. It uses raw .csv data saved to my github repository, taken from the 'FRED' database.

California Wildfires

My sixth chart originally showed the top 5 wildfires in California by size, however I have made edits to the csv file by adding 5 other regions in California with fictional fires. These were Fresno, Ventura, Yuba, Colusa and Mariposa.

Gross Federal Debt

My seventh chart shows Gross Federal Debt in the USA from 2012 to 2019. Instead of using an API or raw csv in my github repository, I have manually input the data points into my json file.

ReMark Holdings Stock Price

This chart shows the intraday stock price of 'ReMark Holdings', a technology company that develops AI products. I chose this specific stock due to its extraordinary volatility on the day this chart was embedded, reaching an all time high on that day. I have used the AlphaVantage API for this respective data. As this is an intraday chart, every 24hrs the chart self updates.
